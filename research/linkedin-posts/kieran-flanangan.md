- Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kieranjflanagan_you-can-turn-your-marketing-team-into-an-share-7475942337764868096-Nz6L
- Summary: Kieran uses David Ogilvy's practice of documenting his advertising judgment in internal memos as an analogy for how marketing teams should work with AI today. He argues that creating an internal knowledge base — writing down what works, what doesn't, and why — turns institutional knowledge into a durable, AI-usable asset. He outlines a 3-step approach: measure AI's impact on real outcomes, teach AI on the team's raw intelligence, and build a documented knowledge base usable by both AI and humans.
